# GPS: A Plain-English Guide to a Regenerative Sovereign Economic Layer

**Working definition for LUCA / Solaris:** GPS can be understood as a **Generative Prosperity System** or **Global Prosperity Split**. It is not the same as map GPS. It is an economic coordination layer that helps route value, identity, data, energy, contribution, reputation, and rewards through a local-first sovereign ecosystem.

The simple idea:

> GPS is a programmable value-routing system that makes every useful transaction feed the person, the provider, the local node, the community, and the regenerative commons - instead of extracting most of the value into a centralized platform.

In the Solaris / LUCA vision, GPS becomes the layer that answers:

- Who is participating?
- What did they contribute?
- Who created the value?
- How should the money, reputation, data rights, and rewards flow?
- How do we make the local ecosystem stronger after each transaction?
- How do humans, businesses, clinics, farmers, builders, hardware operators, and AI agents collaborate without becoming dependent on an extractive platform?

---

## 1. The beginner explanation

Today, most digital platforms work like this:

1. People create value.
2. A platform captures the relationship, data, attention, payment flow, and reputation.
3. The platform becomes the gatekeeper.
4. The people and businesses who created the value become dependent on the platform.
5. Local economies leak value outward.

GPS flips this model.

GPS says:

1. The human owns their identity.
2. The business owns its customer relationship.
3. The local node helps power the infrastructure.
4. AI agents help coordinate work, but do not own the human.
5. Payments route automatically and transparently.
6. Contributors are rewarded when they actually help the ecosystem grow.
7. A small share of activity can flow into a regenerative treasury that funds health, food, education, local compute, open tools, and community resilience.

So instead of building another app, GPS helps build a **sovereign economic operating system**.

## 2. Why GPS matters

GPS matters because humanity has a coordination problem, not only a money problem.

We have many people doing useful work: doctors, dentists, farmers, drivers, educators, healers, developers, designers, local businesses, families, and community builders. But the current internet often fails to connect them in a fair way.

The old model rewards extraction:

- Own the platform.
- Own the user data.
- Own the payment rail.
- Own the marketplace.
- Own the ranking algorithm.
- Own the reputation layer.
- Charge everyone rent forever.

The GPS model rewards contribution:

- Help someone heal, learn, earn, build, host, teach, feed, transport, verify, compute, or coordinate.
- Get rewarded based on the value created.
- Let the local ecosystem become more powerful every time value moves.

GPS is the missing value-accounting and value-routing layer between:

- Identity
- Bitcoin / Lightning / wallet rails
- AI agents
- Local business workflows
- Hardware nodes
- Data vaults
- Health and wealth passports
- Community treasuries
- Regenerative commerce

## 3. The core principle: value should flow to where value was created

GPS is based on one simple law:

> Value should flow back to the people, tools, infrastructure, and communities that actually created the value.

A dental patient does not only pay a clinic. Many contributors may have helped create that successful patient journey:

- The dentist who performs the treatment
- The clinic that coordinates the case
- The AI agent that educates and follows up
- The local server that protects the patient data
- The marketer who brought the patient ethically
- The driver or hotel partner who supports medical tourism
- The regenerative food or health partner who supports recovery
- The open-source software layer that made coordination possible
- The local community treasury that keeps the ecosystem resilient

GPS is the layer that can split, track, and route that value fairly.

The exact percentages can change. The important idea is that value is split transparently and intentionally.

## 5. How energy is transferred correctly

This is the deeper philosophical part.

Energy is not only electricity. Energy also means time, attention, skill, care, knowledge, money, food, computation, and trust.

In extractive systems, energy is captured incorrectly.

A person gives attention, a platform sells it.
A patient gives data, a platform owns it.
A provider gives care, a platform owns the relationship.
A community creates culture, outsiders monetize it.
A worker creates value, the system routes most of it upward.

GPS tries to correct the energy flow.

It does this by combining:

1. **Sovereign identity** - the human is not owned.
2. **Self-custody wallets** - the money is not trapped.
3. **Local-first data** - the data is not extracted by default.
4. **Bitcoin settlement** - savings and value can exist outside fiat debasement.
5. **Programmatic splits** - contributors are paid when value moves.
6. **AI coordination** - complexity becomes manageable.
7. **Hardware nodes** - infrastructure becomes locally owned.
8. **Regenerative treasuries** - the ecosystem funds its own future.

That is how energy transfer becomes more correct:

> The person who creates, hosts, teaches, heals, feeds, protects, verifies, coordinates, or improves the system can receive value back from the system.
